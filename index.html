Treasure Maze

A puzzle game that relaxes and trains mindfulness. The playing field will change with your every move, use the right strategy to get to the treasure as soon as possible
Supported: single mode, Hotseat mode on one device, LAN play up to 4 people. You can save game progress, supported languages: en, es, ru
The application is completely FREE
